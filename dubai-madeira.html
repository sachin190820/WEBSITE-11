The 'Dubai 'Madeira development is a challenge to the Portuguese architecture, with the construction of 400 luxury apartments in the west of Funchal. This will be the largest private investment made in the autonomous region of Madeira.

LIFESTYLE

SURELY ONE OF THE MOST SPECIAL PLACES IN MADEIRA, WHERE YOU CAN SAVOR THE TRUE PLEASURE OF LIFE INSPIRED BY THIS EXOTIC SCENERY AND THE SEA.

Living in 'Dubai 'Madeira is to enjoy the diversity of options between the city environment, restaurants, stores and art centers, and assortment of activities that goes from adventure in the Atlantic, to the largest Laurissilva forest in the world. An idyllic island with a tropical climate to create unique memories.

GOLDEN VISA PROPERTY INVESTMENT PROGRAM

The Portugal Golden Residence Program is one of the most enticing Investment Programs in Europe – a five-year residence-by-investment program for non-EU nationals.

0.01
Why Portugal

  • Cost of living among the lowest in the European Union

  • Portugal is among the six safest countries in the world (Global Peace Index 2022)

  • Dynamic and profitable real estate market

  • Funchal was elected the best city in Europe to raise children (Preply 2022)

  • Support and investment incentives

0.02
Advantages

  • Becoming a European citizen

  • Visa exemption for entry into several countries such as the United States, Canada and Japan

  • The Portuguese Passport is considered the sixth best in the world, allowing entry into 188 countries

  • Access to the best schools and universities in Portugal and Europe

  • Possibility to live and work in Portugal and other Schengen Area countries without a visa, only with registration in the respective member state

0.03
Type of investment

  • Acquisition of real estate with a value equal to or greater than € 500.000, aimed at ou housing purposes, in the Autonomous Regions of the Madeira. More than one property can be acquired to reach the amount of €500,000.

  • Real estate with construction completed at least 30 years ago, or located in urban rehabilitation areas, to undergo rehabilitation works, for a total minimum value of €350,000, free of encumbrances or charges.
