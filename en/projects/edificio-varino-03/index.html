The VARINO 03 Building is located on Avenida 5 de Outubro, next to what was one of the city 's gateways in the 18th century. Contrasting with the history of Lisbon, but perfectly integrated with its various elements, you will find this building attracts by its elegant style and its centrality, in an urban environment surrounded by jacarandas and the nature of the Gulbenkian Foundation Garden, an emblematic space of the modern movement in Portugal and a reference for the Portuguese landscape architecture.
