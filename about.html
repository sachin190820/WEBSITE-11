ABOUT VARINO

VARINO was born from a bold vision and a fusion of knowledge and technical capacity, making us a reference today, as we are marking the beginning of a new era in the real estate industry. Since the early concept up to the conclusion of a project, we bring ease through transparency and flexibility, by providing solutions, cultivating trust and a relationship with the client, who chooses to step forward with us.

WHAT WE HAVE
AND WHAT WE DO

0.01
Experience

We are the result of a constant dynamic interaction  between solid and experienced companies in the market. VARINO is the result of the partnership between the AFA Group and the Socicorreia Group, a real estate developer specialized in luxury properties. We are the outcome of an active and entrepreneurial vision, which intends to continue raising the standards of the real estate sector.

0.02
Passion

We are deeply passionate about what we do. Every day, our teams prove their dedication and commitment to everything they do. The demand for quality and excellence, which goes hand in hand with art and sophistication, unite our people, and our clients, around VARINO real estate projects.

0.03
We find solutions

As creative problem solvers, we break new ground, creating exceptional environments that deliver lifetime value to every investor, partner and client. We believe in a collaborative approach that, through unparalleled design and thoughtful execution, sparks creativity and leads us to the best solutions.

0.04
Make room for Innovation

We embrace innovation and anticipate change in an organic way instead of waiting to adapt to it. We are in a continuous search for new concepts and creative freedom, maintaining the rigor and focus on quality and design that distinguishes us in the national market.
